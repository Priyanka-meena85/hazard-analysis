About The System

Understanding our core purpose and mission

Improving Safety & Reducing Risk

The Hazard Analysis and Ranking System (HARS) is designed to help organizations proactively manage workplace safety. By providing a structured approach to hazard identification and risk assessment, HARS enables teams to make data-driven, informed decisions.

Our goal is to ensure compliance, foster a culture of safety, and reduce the likelihood of incidents through timely corrective actions and priority-based tracking.

Key Features

Everything you need to maintain a safe working environment

Hazard Identification

Easily log and categorize hazards as soon as they are spotted.

Risk Ranking

Automatically rank risks based on calculated severity and probability.

Priority-Based Analysis

Focus resources on resolving the most critical hazards first.

Role-Based Access

Secure access levels for Admins, Managers, and Operators.

Safety Dashboard

Get a bird's-eye view of your organization's safety metrics.

Corrective Actions

Track resolution status in real-time until the hazard is closed.
